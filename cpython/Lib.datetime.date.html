def isocalendar(self):

Return a named tuple containing ISO year, week number, and weekday. The first ISO week of the year is the (Mon-Sun) week containing the year's first Thursday; everything else derives from that. The first week is 1; Monday is 1 ... Sunday is 7. ISO calendar algorithm taken from http://www.phys.uu.nl/~vgent/calendar/isocalendar.htm (used with permission)
