turtle-example-suite: tdemo_planets_and_moon.py Gravitational system simulation using the approximation method from Feynman-lectures, p.9-8, using turtlegraphics. Example: heavy central body, light planet, very light moon! Planet has a circular orbit, moon a stable orbit around the planet. You can hold the movement temporarily by pressing the left mouse button with the mouse over the scrollbar of the canvas.
