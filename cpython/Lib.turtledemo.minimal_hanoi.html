turtle-example-suite: tdemo_minimal_hanoi.py A minimal 'Towers of Hanoi' animation: A tower of 6 discs is transferred from the left to the right peg. An imho quite elegant and concise implementation using a tower class, which is derived from the built-in type list. Discs are turtles with shape "square", but stretched to rectangles by shapesize() --------------------------------------- To exit press STOP button ---------------------------------------
