class Lib._pydecimal.DivisionImpossible(InvalidOperation):

View In Hierarchy

Cannot perform the division adequately. This occurs and signals invalid-operation if the integer result of a divide-integer or remainder operation had too many digits (would be longer than precision). The result is [0,qNaN].
