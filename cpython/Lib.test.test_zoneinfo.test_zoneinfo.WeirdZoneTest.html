def test_zone_very_large_timestamp(self):

Test when a transition is in the far past or future. Particularly, this is a concern if something: 1. Attempts to call ``datetime.timestamp`` for a datetime outside of ``[datetime.min, datetime.max]``. 2. Attempts to construct a timedelta outside of ``[timedelta.min, timedelta.max]``. This actually occurs "in the wild", as some time zones on Ubuntu (at least as of 2020) have an initial transition added at ``-2**58``.
