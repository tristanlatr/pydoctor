class Lib.datetime.timedelta:

View In Hierarchy

Represent the difference between two datetime objects. Supported operators: - add, subtract timedelta - unary plus, minus, abs - compare to timedelta - multiply, divide by int In addition, datetime supports subtraction of two datetime objects returning a timedelta, and addition or subtraction of a datetime and a timedelta giving a datetime. Representation: (days, seconds, microseconds). Why? Because I felt like it.
